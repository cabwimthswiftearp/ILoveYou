THAT LOVE OF THINE


The room was caught in a daze.
Under the orbs that rose to fame,
My whole world fell unders its gaze;
And the stars above hid in shame.

The sun itself wouldn't dare fight,
The comfort of thy warm light.
And with the sweetest whispers of thy embrace,
In peace, my heart has found its place

It once lacked its hue,
And so you made it your cue
To gather a million stars
And heal thousands of my scars

What was once a broken piece
Was patched up by thee,
And the magic gleamed with ease-
For all along though were the key
The light, once again, thou made me see
